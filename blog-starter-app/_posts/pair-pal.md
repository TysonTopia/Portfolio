### What is Pair Pals?
Pair Pals was an app developed for a class in my Computer Science Degree on a team with 4 other people. This app allowed users to link together and raise "Pals", as well as had features and design to encourage more emotionally invested relationships.

This app's design and concept touch on the nostalgia for toys like the Tamagotchi, and add features to help promote more invested relationships by having a shared thing both people care about, and creating shared memories. 

This app had many features built, including a working frontend and backend, in-app purchases, and we even developed a widget for the app, that would let you see the doodles your partner sent to your pal or to you.

### My role

Since this was such a large project to take on, with the art, gameplay, marketing, and research all being included, I actually acted much more in a management/design role in this project. I was consistently making sure other assets were getting coordinated to get done, and making sure that everyone was working towards the same big picture, and that everything contributed not just to the functionality of the app, but also the feel of it.

This would have been an app that, were it released, require people to be able to convince someone else to join them. That is why we focused very much on keeping it not just simple, but visually appealing and using the aesthetic to make people wanting to try it more. Because of how important marketing was, we made a marketing document for Pair Pals.

Lastly, within this project, I also acted as one of the two main faces of the app, presenting it multiple times over the semester, answering questions, and conveying the idea of the app. Within these presentations, I made sure not just to rattle off technical details, but to also emotionally engage with people, building a narrative and story to how I speak, because that is what we were "selling", was emotion and experiences. This had a great success and lead to Pair Pals being one of the best received apps made within the class.
